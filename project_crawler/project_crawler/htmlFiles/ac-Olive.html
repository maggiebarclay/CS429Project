Olive is a normal bear cub villager in the Animal Crossing series. She first appeared in Doubutsu no Mori, and after her appearance in Doubutsu no Mori e+, she remained absent from the series until Animal Crossing: New Leaf - Welcome amiibo. Her catchphrase, "sweet pea," may be related to her name.

In New Horizons, Olive has the nature hobby and may be found reading a book pertaining to an item she is studying, which can be either flowers, bugs, fish, or fossils.

Characteristics[edit]

Artwork of Olive from Animal Crossing.

Appearance[edit]

Olive is a gray bear cub with a tan muzzle. The inside of her ears are hot pink, her eyelids are a lighter shade of gray, while her bangs are outlined by a darker shade of gray. She has tan coloring on the tip of her paws.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the normal personality. For more information, click here.

Olive is a normal villager, which means she will act sweet and mother-like to the player. She will rarely get mad, but she is sensitive, so she may get upset easily.
