Faith is a koala in the Animal Crossing series. She appears in Animal Crossing and Doubutsu no Mori e+ as a normal islander and returns in Animal Crossing: New Horizons as a big sister villager. Her catchphrase, "aloha," is a common Hawaiian greeting.

In New Horizons, Faith has the music hobby and may sing anywhere without the need of a stereo.

Characteristics[edit]

Appearance[edit]

Faith in Animal Crossing.

Faith is an orange koala with dark brown straight-cut bangs, rosy orange cheeks, and a dark orange nose. Her face, arms, and legs have large tan colored markings on them, and she wears a yellow hibiscus tucked behind her ear. She has large eyes with black irises and a wide smile.

Personality[edit]

Animal Crossing[edit]

Below is a brief description of the normal personality. For more information, click here.

As a normal islander, Faith has a peaceful, spacey nature prone to daydreaming and getting lost in thought. In conversation, she frequently reads the player's aura or comments on the natural beauty of the island. Due to her fanciful outlook on life, she is often kind in dealing with the player but can easily wander off into mystical, eccentric, or even nonsensical conversation. She meditates frequently in her pursuit of harmony and tranquility and will request furniture from the player that she believes will improve her meditation.

New Horizons onwards[edit]

Below is a brief description of the big sister personality. For more information, click here.

Faith is a big sister villager, meaning she is very caring, tomboyish and proactive. At times, she is quite blunt, not afraid of sharing her own opinion and speaking her mind. She is very concerned about the player, treating them like her little sibling, as she often gives the player advice about numerous things. Faith is very versatile and has many hobbies, causing her to get along with most villagers, such as jock and lazy villagers. Snooty and cranky villagers, however, have a hard time conversing with her due to their hobby of gossiping and spreading rumors about others which she dislikes.
