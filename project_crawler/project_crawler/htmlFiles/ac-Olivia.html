Olivia is a snooty cat villager in the Animal Crossing series who appears in all games to date.

In New Horizons, Olivia has the music hobby and may sing anywhere without the need of a stereo.

Characteristics[edit]

Artwork of Olivia from Animal Crossing.

Appearance[edit]

Olivia is a white Siamese cat with no visible nose. She has a beauty mark on her left cheek, large eyes with lilac purple eyeliner, and eyelashes that appear to have mascara. Her sclera is yellow with thin, black pupils. Olivia's fur is ivory white, and her ears are rouge with light pink insides. She also has a burgundy-tipped tail, paws, and feet.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the snooty personality. For more information, click here.

Olivia is snooty, which means she is stuck-up and cold, but she may warm up the longer the player gets to know her, although she sometimes spaces off and will not even notice the player is there. She can be cheeky and rude, but she seems to surprise people at times. She is normally obsessed with furniture and clothes, and her house will have plenty of white furnishings from the Regal Series. She will sometimes ask the player to get some for her. As many snooty villagers, she loves Gracie Outfits. Oftentimes she will ask the player to comment on her makeup. Olivia also frequently gossips about other villagers.

In Happy Home Paradise[edit]

In Animal Crossing: New Horizons – Happy Home Paradise, if Eloise lives in the player's town but Olivia does not, then Olivia will be the first client. However, if Olivia also lives there, then the first client will be a specific snooty villager who does not live in the player's town, in this priority order: Julia, Willow, Friga, Blanche, Cleo, Vivian, Purrl, Bree, Blaire, or Tasha.

    Trivia[edit]

    • In a character popularity poll held on the Japanese Animal Crossing website, Olivia ranked as the 15th most popular character at 972 votes, ranking above Goldie and below Copper.
    • Olivia appears on the album cover of "K.K. Bazaar."
