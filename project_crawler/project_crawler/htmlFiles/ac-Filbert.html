Filbert is a lazy squirrel villager in the Animal Crossing series who appears in all games to date. As of Animal Crossing: New Horizons, he is the only lazy squirrel in the series. His name may come from the filbert nut, another name for the hazelnut, which relates to his species, as squirrels are often associated with nuts.

In New Horizons, Filbert has the nature hobby and may be found reading a book pertaining to an item he is studying, which can be either flowers, bugs, fish, or fossils.

Characteristics[edit]

Artwork of Filbert from Animal Crossing.

Appearance[edit]

Filbert is a blue squirrel with a white stripe on his forehead and a white swirl on his tail. He has bright pink cheeks and the inside of his ears are orange.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the lazy personality. For more information, click here.

As a lazy villager, Filbert has a relaxed, laid-back lifestyle and will appear friendly toward the player. He often talks about food and is humorous or naïve during conversations. During conversations with other villagers, he generally gets along well with peppy, smug, and cranky villagers, while snooty and jock villagers may not get along with him due to their conflicting lifestyles. In New Horizons, Filbert goes to sleep at 11 PM and wakes up at 8 AM.
