Rocket is a big sister gorilla villager in the Animal Crossing series. She first appeared in Animal Crossing: New Leaf and has appeared in all subsequent games. Similar to Kid Cat, Agent S, Big Top, and Filly, Rocket is dressed as a superhero.

In New Horizons, Rocket has the fitness hobby and will wear Sporty Shades and lift heavy dumbbells while exercising.

Characteristics[edit]

Appearance[edit]

Artwork of Rocket from New Leaf.

Rocket is a gorilla with yellow fur and tan skin. She wears a pink outfit and helmet that are both emblazoned with a red number 4. Her helmet has white stripes and a teal visor. Her appearance is that of a superhero.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the big sister personality. For more information, click here.

Rocket has a big sister personality, a combination of the snooty, normal, and peppy personalities. Once she becomes a close friend of the player, she will act like a big sister. She may have disputes with snooty and cranky villagers since they enjoy spreading rumors.

    Trivia[edit]

    • Her Japanese name and the shirt she wears represent that she is the fourth superhero introduced in the Animal Crossing series, after Kid Cat, Agent S, and Big Top, in that order, who first appeared in Doubutsu no Mori e+.
    • Breaking the trend followed by Kid Cat, Agent S, and Big Top, Rocket's birthday is not on the fourth day of a month. It is, however, on the fourteenth day of the fourth month of the year.
    • Rocket appears on the album cover of "K.K. Parade."
      • The e-Reader card artwork originally displayed Jane, another gorilla villager, in place of Rocket.
