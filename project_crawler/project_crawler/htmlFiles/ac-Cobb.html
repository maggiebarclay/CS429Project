Cobb is a jock pig villager in the Animal Crossing series. He appears in all games to date except Animal Crossing: Wild World and Animal Crossing: City Folk. His name and green color may be references to Cobb salad, which is made with lettuce and bacon. His catchphrase, "hot dog," refers to his species, as hot dogs are commonly made from pork.

In New Horizons, Cobb has the education hobby and can be seen wearing Rimmed Glasses, reading a novel while outside, and using a magnifying glass when near certain objects. Cobb is the only jock villager with the education hobby.

Characteristics[edit]

Artwork of Cobb from Animal Crossing.

Appearance[edit]

Cobb is a pale green pig with a pink nose and hooves, yellow eyes, and round glasses with black frames. He has an X-shaped bandage on the back of his head, like Octavian. He has a dark pink hue on the inside of his ears and a darker green patch covering his left ear.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the jock personality. For more information, click here.

As a jock villager, Cobb is energetic and has an interest in physical fitness and activity. He often talks about exercise or sports and may brag about his physical fitness. While often friendly to the player, he may comment on their fitness. During conversations with other villagers, he generally gets along well with peppy villagers due their similar hyper personalities and will also get along with smug villagers, while he may offend snooty villagers by critiquing their fitness and annoy cranky villagers with his hyper personality. In New Horizons, Cobb goes to sleep at 12:30 AM and wakes up at 6:30 AM.
