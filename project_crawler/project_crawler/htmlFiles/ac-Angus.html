Angus is a cranky bull villager in the Animal Crossing series. He first appeared in Doubutsu no Mori e+ and has appeared in all subsequent games. His name derives from the "Aberdeen Angus" breed of cow, hence his Scottish catchphrase. It may also be derived from angus beef, a brand of cow beef.

In New Horizons, Angus has the fitness hobby and will wear Sporty Shades and lift heavy dumbbells while exercising.

Characteristics[edit]

Appearance[edit]

Angus in Doubutsu no Mori e+.

Angus is a rusty brown bull that resembles a Hereford cow. He has a white stripe down his nose that gets wider as it nears his tan muzzle. He also has thick eyelashes, some gray hair on his forehead, and big, dark gray horns.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the cranky personality. For more information, click here.

Angus has the cranky personality. At first, he is very rude, but when he and the player become more friendly toward each other, it is revealed that he is kind, and refers to the player as his "only friend". He may mention that he once dated a girl or two in the past. He also mentions a niece of his that is interested in bug collecting.

Villager information[edit]

In Doubutsu no Mori e+[edit]

In Doubutsu no Mori e+, Angus can only move to the player's town if his e-Reader card is scanned. His card was one of six starter cards bundled with the game, alongside Walker, Maggie, Rod, Merry, and Willow.
