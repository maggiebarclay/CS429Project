Gabi is a peppy rabbit villager in the Animal Crossing series who appears in all games to date except Animal Crossing: Pocket Camp.

In New Horizons, Gabi has the fashion hobby and may be seen wearing a pink purse with a white flower on it, as well as a specific headwear or accessory item.

Characteristics[edit]

Artwork of Gabi from Animal Crossing.

Appearance[edit]

Gabi is a white rabbit with a black nose, ten pink freckles (six from New Leaf to amiibo Festival),[nb 3] pink inner ears, and brown feet, paws, and a tail. She also has a tuft of brown hair.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the peppy personality. For more information, click here.

As a peppy villager, Gabi will appear cheerful and friendly. To some people, Gabi can appear rude. She has a short attention span and may have the tendency to overreact about small things. She will be easy to befriend, taking comfort in those she talks to most. She may, however, feel insulted when talking to snooty or cranky villagers, who may gossip about her or disagree with her style. However she gives out comfort to jocks, who are the equivalent to the peppy villagers. She also gets along well with normal, lazy, big sister, and smug villagers.

In New Leaf[edit]

In Animal Crossing: New Leaf, Gabi is the only villager with the "pianist" goal. This is related to her owning a piano in her house in every game prior to Animal Crossing: New Horizons. Additionally, Gabi's house requests in Animal Crossing: Happy Home Designer and Animal Crossing: New Horizons – Happy Home Paradise involve pianos.
