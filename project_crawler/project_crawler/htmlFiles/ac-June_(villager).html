June is a normal bear cub in the Animal Crossing series. She first appeared in Animal Crossing as an islander, and after her appearance in Doubutsu no Mori e+, she remained absent from the series until Animal Crossing: New Leaf - Welcome amiibo, where she returns as a villager.

In New Horizons, June has the nature hobby and may be found reading a book pertaining to an item she is studying, which can be either flowers, bugs, fish, or fossils.

Characteristics[edit]

Appearance[edit]

June in Animal Crossing.

June is a light brown bear cub, whose hair is dark black and has a hibiscus in it, which is orange and yellow in Animal Crossing and recolored red in Animal Crossing: New Leaf - Welcome amiibo. She has blue eyes and a light tan muzzle. The insides of her ears are pink, and the tips of her paws are white.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the normal personality. For more information, click here.

Animal Crossing[edit]

As a normal islander in Animal Crossing, June has a peaceful, spacey nature prone to daydreaming and getting lost in thought. In conversation, she frequently reads the player's aura or comments on the natural beauty of the island. Due to her fanciful outlook on life, she is often kind in dealing with the player but can easily wander off into mystical, eccentric, or even nonsensical conversation. She meditates frequently in her pursuit of harmony and tranquility and will request furniture from the player that she believes will improve her meditation.

New Leaf onwards[edit]

June has the normal personality, which means she will appear kind and motherly towards the player. In New Leaf, she will get along with smug, lazy, peppy, and other normal villagers.

    Trivia[edit]

    • In the Prima Official Guide for Animal Crossing, a render of Jane is erroneously used for June.
    • June appears in the e-Reader card artwork for "K.K. Faire." However, from Animal Crossing: New Leaf onward, the album cover artwork replaces June with Cheri, another bear cub villager.
