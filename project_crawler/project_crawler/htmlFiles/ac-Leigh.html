Leigh is a peppy chicken villager in the Animal Crossing series. She appears in Doubutsu no Mori, Animal Crossing, and Doubutsu no Mori e+. Her name is a pun on the word "lay," as a hen lays eggs.

Characteristics[edit]

Leigh in Doubutsu no Mori.

Appearance[edit]

Leigh is an orange chicken that resembles a Native American, similar to Quetzal, an eagle villager. She has blue-tipped feathers with a native headdress and warpaint.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the peppy personality. For more information, click here.

Leigh is a peppy villager, meaning that she appears happy and excited towards the player and other villagers. She likes receiving new clothes in the mail and can usually be found roaming around the town. Her chipper, energetic nature also allows her to easily befriend other villagers as well as the player, although she will annoy Cranky and Snooty villagers with this personality.
