Bitty is a snooty hippo villager in the Animal Crossing series. She first appeared in Doubutsu no Mori, and after her appearance in Doubutsu no Mori e+, she remained absent from the series until Animal Crossing: New Leaf - Welcome amiibo, returning with several other hippos previously added in New Leaf. Her name may come from "itty-bitty" which is ironic since her species has a large size.

In New Horizons, Bitty has the education hobby and can be seen wearing Rimmed Glasses, reading a novel while outside, and using a magnifying glass when near certain objects.

Characteristics[edit]

Artwork of Bitty from Animal Crossing.

Appearance[edit]

Bitty is a pink hippo with white hooves and half-closed eyes with purple eyeshadow. She has a small, red tuft of hair on the top of her head.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the snooty personality. For more information, click here.

Bitty is a snooty villager. Like all snooty villagers, she can be rude when one first meets her. She will start being friendlier to the player when she gets to know them more. She will also have a huge interest in fashion. She will get along well with cranky and other snooty villagers.
