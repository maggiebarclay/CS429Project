Sasha is a lazy rabbit villager in the Animal Crossing series. He was added to Animal Crossing: New Horizons as part of the 2.0 Free Update on November 4, 2021, and he was added to Animal Crossing: Pocket Camp between the announcement and release of the New Horizons update. His catchphrase, "hoppity," is a nonce variation of the word "hop," which is related to his species.

In New Horizons, Sasha has the fashion hobby and may be seen wearing a brown leather bag, as well as a specific headwear or accessory item. He is the only male villager to have this hobby.

Characteristics[edit]

Appearance[edit]

Sasha is a mint green rabbit with white-tipped ears, white feet, a white muzzle, and a yellow tail. He has large round dark blue eyes with sparkles in them, and a tiny eyelash sticking out of the bottom-outer side of each eye. When he is shocked, his eyes become gray pupils, with a smaller, dark blue pupil in the middle. He has blond bangs on his forehead.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the lazy personality. For more information, click here.

As a lazy villager, Sasha has a relaxed, laid-back lifestyle and will appear friendly toward the player. He often talks about food and is humorous or naïve during conversations. During conversations with other villagers, he generally gets along well with peppy, smug, and cranky villagers, while snooty and jock villagers may not get along with him due to their conflicting lifestyles. In New Horizons, Sasha goes to sleep at 11 PM and wakes up at 8 AM.

In Happy Home Paradise[edit]

In Animal Crossing: New Horizons – Happy Home Paradise, Sasha's moon-themed house request is likely a reference to the Japanese myth of the moon rabbit, in which the dark side of the moon looks like a rabbit making mochi (a Japanese snack associated with the New Year). This house trait is shared with four other rabbit villagers: Carmen's moon-themed house interior in Animal Crossing: City Folk; Dotty's moon-themed house interior in all games except Animal Crossing, Animal Crossing: New Leaf, and Animal Crossing: New Horizons; Mira's moon base-themed house interior in Animal Crossing: New Leaf; and Ruby's moon-themed house interior in all of her game appearances.
