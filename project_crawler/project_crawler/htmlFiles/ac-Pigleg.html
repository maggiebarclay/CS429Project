Pigleg is a jock pig islander in the Animal Crossing series who appears in Animal Crossing and Doubutsu no Mori e+. His name is a portmanteau of pig and pegleg, relating to his pirate-like appearance. His Japanese catchphrase, yōsoro, is a navigational term for watercraft that is analogous to "keep her steady."[1]

Characteristics[edit]

Appearance[edit]

Pigleg is a peach pig with a brown goatee and a white eye patch over his right eye. He has a pink snout and white hooves, and usually has a big smile on his face. He wears a red bandanna.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the jock personality. For more information, click here.

Pigleg is a jock islander. He lives an active lifestyle and loves sports and exercise.

House[edit]

In Animal Crossing[edit]

As an islander, Pigleg's house in Animal Crossing will begin with only one item, and he will request specific items for the player give to him, which he will place in his house. They are the same items that Fruity requests, except for different wallpaper and flooring.
