Azalea is a snooty rhinoceros villager in the Animal Crossing series. She appears in Doubutsu no Mori e+ and Animal Crossing: New Horizons. Her name is a type of flower, referring to the flower markings around her horn and on the back of her head. Her catchphrase, "merci," is French for "thank you."

In New Horizons, Azalea has the nature hobby and may be found reading a book pertaining to an item she is studying, which can be either flowers, bugs, fish, or fossils.

Characteristics[edit]

Appearance[edit]

Azalea in Doubutsu no Mori e+.

Azalea is a light blue rhinoceros with a white flower around her yellow horn. Another white flower is on the back of her head. Her lower jaw is white. She wears pink eyeshadow and is very pale. She has gray, tired eyes, and the inside of her ears match her eyeshadow, which is pink.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the snooty personality. For more information, click here.

Azalea has a snooty personality. She will often mock other villagers. She will appear rude and mean to the player at first. She will like cranky villagers, due to their similar rude personalities. Azalea however, will not get along with jock villagers due to them questioning her fitness. She will also not get along with lazy villagers, since the lazy villager won't care about his physical appearance angering her.

In New Horizons[edit]

Azalea was added to Animal Crossing: New Horizons in the 2.0 Free Update on November 4, 2021. Despite her official New Horizons artwork depicting her wearing the Blossom Dress (Purple), Azalea wears the Sleeveless Tunic (Purple) in-game.
