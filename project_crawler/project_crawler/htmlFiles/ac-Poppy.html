Poppy is a normal squirrel in the Animal Crossing series. She first appeared in Animal Crossing: City Folk and has appeared in all subsequent games. Her name comes from the poppy flower, referring to her fur color. Her catchphrase, "nutty," refers to her species, as squirrels are commonly associated with nuts.

In New Horizons, Poppy has the education hobby and can be seen wearing Rimmed Glasses, reading a novel while outside, and using a magnifying glass when near certain objects.

Characteristics[edit]

Appearance[edit]

Artwork of Poppy from City Folk.

Poppy is a red squirrel with a cream muzzle, tail stripe, and tipped paws. Her ears are turquoise blue on the inside, and her nose is orange.

Personality[edit]

Below is a brief description of the normal personality. For more information, click here.

Poppy has a normal personality, which means she is very friendly, and likes to talk to the player. She rarely gets mad, even if hit with a net. But doing so may make her depressed. She will act motherly to the player and other villagers. She will act sweet and hospitable to the player.
